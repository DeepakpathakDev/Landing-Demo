About StockInsight

We're on a mission to democratize financial data and empower investors with powerful, intuitive tools for making informed decisions.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, StockInsight was born from a simple observation: while financial data was abundant, truly useful insights were scarce and often inaccessible to everyday investors.

Our founders, a team of financial analysts and software engineers, set out to build a platform that would not only aggregate market data but transform it into actionable intelligence through advanced analytics and intuitive visualization.

Today, StockInsight serves thousands of investors worldwide, from individual traders to institutional portfolio managers, all united by the desire to make smarter, data-driven investment decisions.

2020

StockInsight founded in San Francisco with seed funding of $2.5M

2021

Launch of beta platform with real-time data for US markets

2022

Series A funding of $12M; expansion to global markets

2023

Introduction of AI-powered market insights and predictions

2024

Reached 100,000 active users; launched institutional platform
